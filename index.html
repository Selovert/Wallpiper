Features

  • Download wallpapers from interfacelift.
  • Wallpapers are automatically dimensioned for your dekstop
  • Read about the wallpapers you've downloaded

How to Use

To fetch a new wallpaper, click the Wallpiper icon in the menu bar and select 'Get New Wallpaper'.

Once the download has begun, the icon should indicate download progress , followed by a notification that the process has been completed.
